About Me
Hi! I'm Jolin Li

I'm a designer and storyteller currently studying at the Stratford School of Interaction Design and Business. I'm passionate about crafting digital experiences that are both intuitive and emotionally resonant.

At the University of Waterloo, I've been exploring the intersection of technology, creativity, and human connection, learning how thoughtful design can shape the way people interact with the world and each other. My work blends clean aesthetics with meaningful function, whether I'm prototyping interfaces, designing visual identities, or curating digital content.

Outside of the classroom, I draw inspiration from fashion, photography, and everyday conversations. I believe that great design starts with empathy and grows through collaboration.

Let's connect and create something impactful.

Study Spot Finder


Our team designed a high-fidelity wireframe for a study space app that features real-time availability updates, customizable filters, and collaborative event planning. Tailored for students who face challenges finding ideal study spots during busy hours, the prototype was developed in Figma to enhance user experience and accessibility on campus.

Video Streaming App


This app was developed in just two hours as part of an in-class Figma design challenge. The task was to create a functional and visually engaging video streaming app that met a specific set of user and technical requirements. Despite the time constraint, the final product included user-friendly navigation, personalized recommendations, and a clean layout designed to enhance the viewing experience.
